Project #1: Pricio
Full Stack Developer - Swift
Venezuela's hyperinflation is over 1,000,000%.
Businesses cannot afford to continuously print
new price tags, as prices rise by the hour. As if
that wasn't enough, people are forced to wait
in lines to ask for prices at the cashier, which
creates disorder and stress.
No more price tags, lines and disorder.
With Pricio, users have it easier than ever. They just pull up their
phones, point it to the product's barcode, and get the price all in
real time. They can also add such product to their cart, letting
them control the total amount and adjust to their needs.
Admins also benefit, as they are able to quickly add and delete
products, as well as editing existing ones. They can also modify
the exchange rate that defines the product's prices in real time.
Project #2: GatorRater
Full Stack Developer - MERN Stack
Gator Rater is a website built to help University of Florida
students maintain mental wellness. It was built using the
the MERN stack. It has 4 features soon to be explained.
I worked as a full stack developer, designing the entire UI,
building the comment board, resource page, and more.
Gator Rater won the Best Project Award
#1 out of 46 contestants
Features
Daily Popup
Every 24 hours, once the user logs in, they get a form that
asks how they felt that day. This data is saved and plotted
on the dashboard's graphs, which let the user track their
wellness, and seek help if necessary. It targets important
factors for wellness such as sleep, exercise and stress.
Weekly Schedule
Users upload iCal files that lay out the schedule for the week.
They can also rate or check the rating of such assignments,
calculated by averaging all ratings by users that completed
them. Ratings are based on difficulty and time to complete.
This feature lets users plan ahead and reduce stress.
Comment Board
Users can check comments for classes they plan to take, this
gives them a better insight on what to expect and plan ahead.
They can also filter by specific professors teaching the course,
as well as leaving their own comment, liking, flagging, etc.
Lastly, each search has a direct link to relevant Reddit forums.
Resources Page
Users can see a map with locations that target different
aspects of overall wellness. Users can also suggest new places
to add to the map, and new tips for other users after being
accepted by the admins. Lastly, users can see a list of
hotlines and websites where they can get quick help.
